2/5/2015

So far my take on the DBC eperience has been pretty smooth and problem free. I did prepare on my own a little bit before starting phase 0. What I anticipate is a very difficult, but inspired change in me, and I hope this is realized. I will try my best to do whatever it takes for this to happen on my part. I already have been inspired to become a programmer. I have created tools that were programming oriented that I felt very driven to create and make better. I look to a career in programming to foster this feeling even in my daily life, in the hopes that I will build and be a great contributor to some common good, as well as my own interests. If DBC can give capture that feeling allow others to feel that way, I believe that this is a great goal. I am definitely excited for this to happen.

There are several aspects of DBC that I find beneficial. I like the aspect of working together to learn from each other. I have always been team orriented but I understand there is some benefit to struggling with material individually as well.

I really hope I can engage and contribute to my cohort to not dissappoint them and give the the best learning experience that they deserve as well.

What has made me nervous is falling behind in the material because it is so fast paced and not being able to contribute, or even being asked to leave. I am also nervous about opening up and expressing my inabilities and asking for help. But, I know these are all necessary for my success at DBC and even career and life.
