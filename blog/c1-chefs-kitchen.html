So far my take on the DBC experience has been pretty smooth and problem free. I did prepare on my own a little bit before starting phase 0. What I anticipate is a very difficult and inspired change in me, and I hope this is realized. I will try my best to do whatever it takes for this to happen on my part. I already have been inspired to become a programmer. I have created tools that were programming orriented that I felt very driven to create and make better. I look to a career in programming to foster this feeling, even in my daily life. I hope that I will build things and be a great contributor to some common good, as well as my own interests. If DBC can capture that feeling and help others to feel that way, I believe that this is a great goal. I am definitely excited for this to happen.

There are several aspects of DBC that I find beneficial. One aspect I like the aspect of working together to learn from each other. I have always been team orriented, but I understand there is some benefit to struggling with material individually as well and strecthing yourself.

I really hope I can engage and contribute to my cohorts education to not dissappoint them. I'd like to give the the best learning experience that they deserve as well.

What has made me nervous is falling behind in the material because it is so fast paced and not being able to contribute, or even being asked to leave. I am also nervous about opening up and expressing my inabilities and asking for help. But, I know these are all necessary for my success at DBC and even career and life.
