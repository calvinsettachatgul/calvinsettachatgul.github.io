Data Structures Overview 04/12/15

I've just had revelations and refreshers about data structures. After taking a couple courses on datastructures in Java and C++. I just had an AHA! moment about how important they are in how an application works. While taking the courses and getting an introduction to them I think I was in the cocktail party realm of knowledge about their imporatance. Of course I had knowledge about how to implement the data structures and their associated objects and behaviors, but really I didn't know the significance of how a data structure can impact funtionality and performance.

Just imagine the vast store of data that a startup has to store for user accounts and their associated data. Taking that even further what about the mind boggling data that a company such as Google has sort through when implementing a search over the internet. In these situations data sorting and searching is continually being optimized. I imagine internet bots roaming and searching the internet to find out what my webpage is about and then somehow referencing it if this page were searchable. Over time those bots collect a vast ammount of references on different topics. That's huge amount of searching for keyword strings.

To have a great cocktail party conversation or geeky happy-hour get together about data structures several key concepts should be taken note of.
